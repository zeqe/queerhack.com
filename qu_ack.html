About

More than just a hackathon

queer_hack is a community for LGBTQ+ students in tech based out of UNC Chapel Hill.

Vision

We envision a future with a tech culture that is inclusive and accessible for LGBTQ+ people.

Mission

We aim to empower LGBTQ+ students in tech by fostering peer connections and curating opportunities to grow as a programmer.

Our event programming includes skill-building workshops, weekly study groups, social events, career networking opportunities, and an annual hackathon.


Whether you’re already a Computer Science major or just interested in exploring coding, we’d love for you to join the community.

queer_hack 2020: Our Inaugural Hackathon
Friday December 4 - Sunday December 6, 2020
Registration will open on Monday October 26th

Whether you’ve never coded before or you’re a senior Computer Science major who has already scored a software engineering job offer, all LGBTQ+ students are welcome at queer_hack 2020. One of the first hackathons by and for LGBTQ+ students in the nation, queer_hack 2020 will feature workshops, speakers, social events, and a coding competition. Join us to try something new, connect with LGBTQ+ peers and professionals, and sharpen your programming skills.
